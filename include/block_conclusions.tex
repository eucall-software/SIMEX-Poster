\block[titleoffsety=20pt,bodyoffsety=20pt,bodyoffsetx=0pt]{Conclusions}{%
  \sloppy\small%
  \begin{itemize}
    \item Start--to--end simulations enable studying impact of various artifacts
      and parameters on experimental data:
      \begin{itemize}
        \item Source fluctuations and beamline optics
        \item Target/sample structure and dynamics (radiation damage)
        \item Incoherent scattering
        \item Detector response
      \end{itemize}
    \item We optimized the target geometry of Fe--CH sandwich targets for laser
      shock compression experiments using 1D radiation--hydrodynamics
      simulations
    \item  Simulated diffraction from hydrated proteins gives estimate of tolerable
      water layer thickness $\Rightarrow$ $R < 0.2$ criterion indicates that \SI{3}{\angstrom} water layer permits
      sub--nm resolution imaging.
  \end{itemize}
}%
